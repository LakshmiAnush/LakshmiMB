About Me

With a B.Tech and M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering and nearly 7 years of professional experience across academia, research, and industry, I have contributed in diverse roles — from Graduate Trainee at VSSC, where I participated in aerospace R&D, to Assistant Professor mentoring engineering students, and PG Diploma Tutor guiding advanced technical subjects. Currently, I focus on teaching coding and providing software consultancy, helping individuals and businesses leverage technology effectively. I am passionate about software development, AI-powered solutions, and mentoring the next generation of tech enthusiasts. In addition, I am building hands-on experience in Generative AI, Large Language Models (LLMs), and prompt engineering, with practical projects in Python and machine learning. My mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications, empowering learners and professionals to thrive in the rapidly evolving technology landscape.
